# Employee Handbook — Acme Corp

## Office Location

The main office is located at 42 Innovation Drive, Suite 800, San Francisco, CA 94107.
Remote employees should use the registered address for tax purposes.

## Annual Leave

Full-time employees receive 25 days of annual leave per calendar year.
Leave accrues at a rate of 2.08 days per month.
Unused leave may be carried over up to a maximum of 10 days.

## Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted twice per year: in March and in September.
Each review includes a self-assessment, a manager assessment, and a calibration session.
Outcomes are tied to compensation adjustments effective the following quarter.

## Equipment Policy

All employees receive a company-issued laptop on their first day.
Personal devices must not be used to access internal systems without prior IT approval.
Equipment must be returned within 5 business days of separation.

## Health Benefits

Medical, dental, and vision coverage begins on the first day of the month following hire.
The company covers 80% of the individual premium and 60% of dependent premiums.
Employees may enroll in an HSA-compatible plan or a PPO plan.

## Code of Conduct

Employees are expected to act with integrity in all professional interactions.
Harassment, discrimination, or retaliation of any kind is grounds for immediate termination.
Concerns should be reported to HR or via the anonymous ethics hotline at 1-800-555-0199.
